Welcome to PHENIX

Phoenix Worldwide Movers is a UAE based organization specialized in Door to Door domestic and International Moving activities. We takes the stress out of the moving process by providing a seamless and worry-free experience. Professionals with a proven track record and extensive experience manage the company in the business of removal and packing throughout the UAE. We carry out hundreds of removals from Dubai wide each year, and are proud to be the movers that UAE businesses and expatriates turn to when in need of a comprehensive service that takes the fuss out of relocating. We understand that no two moves are alike. Therefore we view every move as a custom-tailored solution with a personal touch. Unlike other movers in Abu Dhabi and Dubai, we have the resources and expertise to treat your property as though it were our own. Every item is packed to ensure the maximum protection of the contents. In addition to that, special care is given to delicate pieces, large works, pianos, etc. by the use of professional lashing and crating.

Why Choosing us?

Customer Satisfaction

In Phoenix Worldwide Movers, customer satisfaction is our main goal and in order to achieve, maintain and continuously, increase the satisfaction rate, we have set up very strict removal quality policies based on various standards and on customer feed back.

Customer Feedback

Customer feedback is vital to achieve our aim of complete customer satisfaction. At each step of removal process, we ask our clients opinion about our service for analysis and will take all necessary corrective/preventive measures to improve our service levels based on your feedback. Through this exercise, we ensure continual improvement to our services and customer experience. Apart from the above, our highly experienced move coordinators are always available on phone or via e-mail in order to assist you for any queries that may arise during your whole removal process. Through this, we guarantee 100 % complete satisfaction to our customers.

Communication

We understand that communicating to our client is the key to understanding to their needs. As our clients come from all part of the world, we employ multi-cultural staffs who are able to communicate in many languages to remove any language barriers. This is particularly to help the expatriates while preparing for their international moves.

Customer Service

IThe essence of good customer service is forming a relationship with customers a relationship that that individual customer feels that he would like to pursue. Our staffs always understand that reliability is one of the keys to any good relationship and they always ensure to keep the customers happy by giving them proper and adequate information about the services that you would require us to undertake.

Security & Confidentiality

At Phoenix, we are always committed to ensure the safety & security of all the consignments in our care. Every effort is made to protect the removal site, products, materials, work & buildings. Our crew are specially trained to undertake all responsible precautions to prevent unauthorized access to the work site to prevent damage, theft etc. In addition, we ensure that your personal information are stored in our management system with high confidentiality. We also ensure that the information on your personal profile / inventory is been accessed and used only at the point of customs clearance at origin/destination.
